\subsection{Evaluation}
When assessing the models, the data was divided such that 80\% of the data was used for training and 20\% for testing. 
This totals to approximately 4364 training instances and 1092 testing instances. As can be seen in figure Y, the dataset mainly consisted of Drama and Comedy, followed by Action, then Adventure, Horror, and Crime. 
All following genres have approximately less than 200 instances. 
Considering the imbalance in the dataset, weighted f1-score was used as the primary metric for evaluation. 
The secondary evaluation metric was accuracy to measure the proportion of correct predictions of each model.
