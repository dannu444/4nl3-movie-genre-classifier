\subsection{Zero-Shot Model (Flan-T5)}
Overall, the performance of Flan-T5 is impressive given it was given no training and the biases that exist within the dataset.
If work was to continue on this model, it is recommended that some degree of fine-tuning is attempted, as well as further prompt experimentation. 
Fine tuning may allow for the model to adapt to the inherent bias in the dataset discussed within Implementation, although a larger corpus of descriptions would need to be compiled. 
Prompt experimentation, as well as investigation into the larger Flan-T5 models with more parameters, may prove useful for optimization and overall performance respectively.
